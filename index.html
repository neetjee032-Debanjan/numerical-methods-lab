Introduction to Data Analytics

Data Analytics is the science of examining raw data to extract meaningful insights, patterns, and conclusions that support decision-making. It combines statistics, programming, and domain knowledge to transform data into actionable intelligence.

In today’s digital world, every click, transaction, and interaction produces data. Data analytics helps organizations understand customer behavior, improve performance, reduce risks, and predict future outcomes.
